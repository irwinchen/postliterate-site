Explaining Post-Literacy

Three tiers of reading, each as a share of total world population, against the events that shaped them and the population they ride on. Basic literacy is near-universal; the deep-reading elite is small and flat; the engaged-reading middle rose with mass schooling and is now contracting. Hover events and the population band for detail.
