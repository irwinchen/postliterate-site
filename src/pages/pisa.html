Morais's argumentative literacy aligns with PISA Level 4: 24% of OECD 15-year-olds reached it in 2022, down from 29% in 2015. PISA's own "baseline" (Level 2) sits two rungs below that threshold.

The empirical pivot: argumentative literacy (Morais's Level 2) maps to PISA Level 4. 24% of OECD 15-year-olds reached Level 4 or above in 2022.
