*Is it okay to [use AI to help me study](https://www.chronicle.com/special-projects/the-different-voices-of-student-success/ai-to-the-rescue)? 
Is it okay for [me to make an outline for a paper and then have AI write it for me](https://jasmi.news/p/the-post-literate-society)?
Is it okay for teachers to [use AI to help them prepare tests](https://www.edweek.org/technology/should-teachers-disclose-when-they-use-ai/2024/04)? 
Is it  okay for teachers to [use AI to write a recommendation letter](https://s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/craft-resource-files/AI-Admissions-Key-Findings_Final_2024.07.08.pdf)? 
Is it okay to [use AI to generate artwork that I use as reference for my own painting](https://cacm.acm.org/news/ai-and-art/)? 
Is it okay to [use AI to summarize a paper I'll never read myself](https://www.technologynetworks.com/informatics/articles/your-favorite-ai-chatbot-might-be-exaggerating-scientific-findings-399980)?*
